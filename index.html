Welcome! This is a simple, well-structured HTML template focused on good SEO basics: semantic markup, meta tags, Open Graph/Twitter cards, canonical URL, and structured data. Replace text, titles, and URLs with your site-specific content.

Why these SEO elements matter

  • Title & meta description appear in search results and influence click-through rate.
  • Canonical prevents duplicate-content indexing issues.
  • Structured data (JSON-LD) helps search engines understand the page and may enable rich results.
  • Open Graph & Twitter tags control how the page looks when shared on social media.

Quick checklist

  1. Customize the <title>, meta description, and canonical URL.
  2. Use a unique H1 per page and include target keywords naturally.
  3. Ensure images have descriptive alt text and are optimized for size.
  4. Serve pages over HTTPS and make sure they are crawlable (no auth, noindex removed).
